About Taiko

A UK-registered exporter built on
trust, traceability and documentation.

Taiko Food and Beverage Ltd was founded to solve a specific problem: buyers in Lagos want genuine UK products, and it is hard to know who to trust. Counterfeit and repackaged goods are common in the Nigerian market, particularly in personal care, spirits and baby products.

Our focus is documentation. We photograph and record every consignment at the point of purchase in the UK, verify expiry dates case by case, and keep the paper trail from wholesaler invoice to Lagos delivery. Buyers can see where their goods came from: the company is based in Northfleet, Kent, and works with established UK cash and carry and wholesale suppliers. Goods are consolidated into palletised consignments and shipped by sea to Lagos, where they are cleared and delivered to our warehouse network and wholesale relationships.

We are a small company and we value transparency. What we offer is not scale—it is traceability, honest stock, and a direct line to the person who bought the goods. Taiko was started by Wole, who runs the buying side in the UK personally. If you order from us, the person who picked your stock off the shelf is the person answering your messages.
