What We Do

We Source. We Document. We Deliver.

We buy directly from established UK wholesalers and ship by sea freight to Lagos. Every consignment travels with complete documentation: supplier invoices, batch codes, expiry verification and the relevant export certificates.

Our range covers premium bottled water, spirits, vitamins and supplements, baby care, personal care, and branded food staples.

Verified Sourcing

All goods are purchased from UK trade suppliers. We record batch codes and expiry dates for every case before it leaves the warehouse.

Full export documentation

EORI-registered exporter. DEFRA certificates of free sale obtained where required. Documentation prepared for NAFDAC registration and Nigeria's NICIS II single window.

Sea freight to Lagos

Palletized consignments shipped on a scheduled basis. Pre-orders accepted against upcoming shipments.
